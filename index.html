How to Make a Grilled Cheese Sandwich

  1. Gather bread and cheese.
  2. Heat George Foreman grill.
  3. Assemble sandwich.
  4. Place sandwich on heated grill.
  5. Toast the sandwich until golden brown and cheese is melted.
  6. Carefully remove from grill and allow to cool.
  7. Enjoy!
